# subtrack-privacy

 Privacy Policy
    The FlowSubs application is designed to help users manage their subscription tracking.
    
    Data Collection
    FlowSubs does not collect or store personal information externally. Subscription data is stored locally on your device.
    
    Advertising
    The app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect anonymous device identifiers to show relevant ads.
    
    Notifications
    The app may send local notifications to remind users of upcoming subscription payments.
    
    Third-Party Services
    The app uses the following third-party service: Google AdMob

    Data Sharing
    FlowSubs does not sell, share, or transmit your personal data to third parties.
    
    Changes
    This privacy policy may be updated from time to time.
